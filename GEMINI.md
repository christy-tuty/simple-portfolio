# Project Overview: iPhone 17 Research & Comparison

This directory serves as a focused research hub for the rumored **iPhone 17** series, specifically comparing the **iPhone 17 (Base)** and the **iPhone 17 Air**. The goal is to provide data-driven recommendations based on specific user personas, such as social media content creators, content consumers, and heavy AI (Gemini) users.

## Directory Overview

The project organizes rumored specifications, market positioning, and usage-based analysis to help users decide which upcoming model fits their lifestyle. It places a particular emphasis on:
- **AI Performance:** RAM and processor optimizations for Apple Intelligence and Gemini.
- **Social Media Workflow:** Camera versatility and display technology for creators and consumers.
- **Physical Design:** The trade-offs between the new "ultra-thin" design and battery/hardware capacity.

## Key Files

- **`models_comparison.md`**: The master comparison document. It contains:
    - A detailed specification table (Display, RAM, Camera, etc.).
    - A "Social Media Benefit" analysis integrated into the technical specs.
    - Summary of rumored key changes (e.g., 24MP selfies, 120Hz ProMotion).
    - Detailed recommendations for different user profiles.
- **`nothing_model.md`**: A concise summary of the recommendation logic, focusing on the distinction between **Content Creators** (favoring the Base model) and **Content Consumers** (favoring the Air model).
- **`Data/`**: A placeholder directory for future research data, raw specs, or additional source documents.

## Usage Guidelines

- **Consult `models_comparison.md` first**: This is the primary source of truth for all technical specifications and the latest analysis.
- **Persona-Based Advice**: When giving advice, refer to the distinction between "Creators" (who need the dual-camera system) and "Consumers" (who benefit from the larger screen).
- **AI Context**: Always factor in the **12GB RAM** and **A19 Pro** rumors for users asking about Gemini or AI performance, as the "Air" model is positioned as the AI-optimized choice.
- **Maintaining Accuracy**: Update the comparison table as new rumors or official announcements surface to ensure the "Rumored" status is updated.
